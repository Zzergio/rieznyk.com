Who is Mr. Rieznyk?

Hi, my name is Sergio and I'm a web designer from Ukraine. Nice to meet you on my page.

I offer modern responsive UX/UI design for websites and web apps.

I am happy to help you if your business needs a quality website or application. My mission is to make clear and high-quality UX/UI design for online stores, landing pages, business websites that meets all customer demands. I live and work in a magnificent country - Ukraine. For over a decade I have been doing what I love. During this time I have gained experience working on more than a hundred projects of different complexity and subjects. I prefer to create websites and applications in a minimalist style as clear as possible to the user.

In my work I specialize in using both raster and vector graphics (Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Corel Draw). And Adobe Photoshop is my best friend. My key strengths are 10 years of experience, 100% reliability, WhatsApp calls availability, sharp compliance with deadlines.
